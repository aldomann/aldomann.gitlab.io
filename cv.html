Experience

Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona

Lead Statistical Programmer & Project Manager • July 2018 — Present

In the frame of RENEB, I am developing Biodose Tools, an Open Source R Shiny application to help professionals and researchers on Biological Dosimetry to perform several computations and statistical analyses.

Our end-goal is to replace all current software used by the different laboratories and to standardise the used statistical methods, which will have a major impact in Interlaboratory Comparison tests for Quality Assurance.

As part of the project, I am writing a comprehensive User Manual & Statistical Documentation book using bookdown.

Centre de Recerca Matemàtica

Complex Systems Intern • September 2017 — July 2018

My Master's Thesis (with Álvaro Corral and Isabel Serra as my thesis supervisors) consisted of building statistical methods using bootstrap and permutation tests to study in depth the influence of sea surface temperature on tropical-cyclone intensity.

Additionally, I created an R package with unified hurricane occurrences dataset with finer temporal temperature tracking than those currently available in the literature.
